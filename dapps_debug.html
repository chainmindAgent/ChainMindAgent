About SERAPH In The Darkness

What is SERAPH In The Darkness?

The SOUL series is a collection of right-based NFTs, as an integral part of the SERAPH world, holding abundant benefits in the forthcoming future, reflecting the long-lasting creativity of the SERAPH IP. It mainly features human heroes such as Barbarians, Valkyries, Sorceresses, Necromancers, along with a few Angels, Demons, and otherworldly races, showcasing the many story characters developed in the game so far. This Unique NFT series is part of the core NFT lineup in SERAPH, providing the capability to secure even rarer in-game drops.
